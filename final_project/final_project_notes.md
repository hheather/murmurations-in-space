## Ideas

### Exploring methods of artistic intervention in the generative processes of GANs.
Explore, break and tweak, or otherwise intervene in the generative process
* Latent space search: Exploring a neural network’s internal representation for interesting artefacts.
* Cross-domain training: Forcefully mixing two training sets, creating outputs which combine features into cross-over outputs.
* Loss-hacking: Manipulating a generator's loss function to divert it towards sub-optimal results.

Inspo: https://computationalcreativity.net/iccc20/papers/164-iccc20.pdf

#### Final output: 
* UI for exploring and tweaking the generated output in real time.
* Collecting outputs into an animation, e.g. moving between two points in a latent space.

#### Questions:
* Which model? Something not too big..
* What input? Own images/train with images of a particular subject.
